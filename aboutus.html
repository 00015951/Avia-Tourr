About Company

Your vacation begins with a visit to us!

Max Avia Tour company was founded in 2006. And for more than 14 years it has provided an amazing opportunity to travel in different directions, visit high countries and helps you choose any type of vacation - from traditional, with beaches and spa resorts, health and educational to exotic and extreme. Max Avia Tour employees will help you choose a comfortable service, decide on the comfort of air travel, and also suggest where is the best place to relax and which hotel is the most pleasant to stay at. We offer a wide range of services: from obtaining visas to organizing individual or group tours, booking tickets, hotels, transfers and organizing excursion and entertainment programs. Our tour operators are ready to choose the second option for you, determine the best-priced and most interesting tours, select the clients wishes, promptly help with paperwork, and also monitor the quality of his vacation, monitoring at all stages of the trip. The most important thing for us is to organize the highest quality holiday, so we work only with proven and reliable employees in the tourism sector.
